Manali

Manali, a picturesque hill station in the Indian state of Himachal Pradesh, is known for its stunning natural beauty and serene environment. Nestled in the Beas River Valley, it offers breathtaking views of snow-capped mountains, lush green forests, and gushing rivers. Manali is a popular destination for adventure enthusiasts, offering activities like trekking, paragliding, and skiing
